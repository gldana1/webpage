Category Analysis

In our third page we have two drop down menus that filter the rest of the page. A matrix on the top left breaks down the different job titles by category. Comparison with the previous year and rate of increase is displayed as a aline in the top right visual. There is also a histogram with the salary distribution broken down in bins of 50k euro each


Statistical Analysis

In our fourth page we filter the outliers of our dataset using a simple formula that filters out the vaules that are more than 2 times the mean plus or minus 2 standard deviations. We show the difference in the forecasted values according to the orignal and the corrected sample using a toggle button. Outliers in our data eg. very high entry level salaries or very low director level salaries.


Geographical Analysis

In our fifth page we start our Geographical analysis and display two factors that paint our maps according to their values. The left one is the average remote ratio for the employees and the right one is a median value of the salaries. Again selecting a country from the map or the matrix displays only that information.
